KIM TAEHYUNG


Kim Tae-hyung (Korean: 김태형; born December 30, 1995),also known professionally as V (Korean: 뷔), is a South Korean singer and a member of the boy band BTS.Since his debut with the group in 2013, V has released three solo tracks under their name—"Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. He appeared on the soundtrack for the television series Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth in 2016 and released his first independent song, the self-composed "Scenery", in 2019.
