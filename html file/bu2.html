BUTTER

THE 7TH MINI ALBUM

BTS released their CD single 'Butter' on July 9. The CD single includes the shimmering summer song “Butter” as well as “Permission to Dance” that will get your heart pumping and dance again. The instrumental versions of each are also part of the tracklist.

TBelonging to the dance pop genre, “Permission to Dance” sits on piano and string sounds that make the listeners bop their heads and bust some dance moves. World-renowned musician Ed Sheeran and British producer Steve Mac took part in making the song.
